Quick Start

Three steps to apply

Send a single email — we take it from there.

  • 1
    Prepare your materials

    CV, a 1–2 page cover letter, and 2–3 references.

  • 2
    Pick your project(s)

    Indicate your preferred Project(s) A–E.

  • 3
    Email the PI

    Send to euiheon.chung@gist.ac.kr.

Application

What to send

Email your application to Prof. Euiheon Chung at euiheon.chung@gist.ac.kr.

Step 1 · Documents

Your application package

  • CV (including publication list)
  • A brief cover letter indicating your preferred Project(s) (A–E) and research interests (1–2 pages)
  • Contact information for 2–3 references
Step 2 · Subject line

Use this exact format

[REMAP Fellowship] Your Name – Project X

Replace “Your Name” and “Project X” (A–E) accordingly. This helps us route your application to the right mentor.

Step 3 · Timeline

Rolling review

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis until all positions are filled. Early applications are encouraged.
